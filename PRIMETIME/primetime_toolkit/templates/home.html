Welcome to The Prime Time Toolkit by Bec Wilson

This Website is based on Bec Wilson's book The Prime Time: 27 Lessons for the new midlife. We are helping Australians aged 47–65 plan smarter for retirement. Take Control of Your Financial Future and join thousands of Australians taking the first step toward confident retirement planning.

☝ We prepared a step-by-step approach you can follow to simplify your pre-retirement journey. Let's get you started!

Step 1

Start with filling out a set of questions in our Self-Assessment tool. Simply click the below link Self-Assessment.

>> Go to Self-Assessment

Step 2

After you submitted the Self-Assessment, you will receive personalized feedback on your financial readiness and get access to our Excel spreadsheet tool via email.

Step 3

After you have received our email, simply upload your completed Excel spreadsheet by clicking the below link. This will provide you with a comprehensible overview.
